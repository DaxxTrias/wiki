Recovery Is Possible (RIP) [Linux](Linux "wikilink") rescue system is a
[Live CD](Live_CD "wikilink") with a number of recovery applications
such as [TestDisk](TestDisk "wikilink"),
[PhotoRec](PhotoRec "wikilink"), etc.
